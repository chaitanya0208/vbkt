 Datta Jayanti Utsav  
  Payee Dindi and Prachar Dindi Sohala Every Year on the ocassion of Datta Jayanti Started From Tryambakeshwar (Nashik) To Shirur.
 
  This utsav is celebrated with all its glory.Every year the week before Datta Jayanti The Dindi Start There Journy From Tryambakeshwar (Nashik) To Shirur.All The devotee gather at Tryambakeshwar and start Payee Dindi with continously chanting Mantr
||OM DATTA DATTA RAM RAM || OM DATTA DATTA RAM || OM SHIV SHIV SHYAM SHYAM || OM SHIV SHIV SHYAM SHYAM ||
 
  On The occasion of Datta Jayanti Sansthan distributes Mahaprasada to all the devotees. Lots of devotees takes advantage of Mahaprasad.
